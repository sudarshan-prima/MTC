About Us आमच्याबद्दल

Mayureshwar Trading Company is a licensed commission merchant (अडतिया / Adatiya) operating at the Jalna APMC Mondha.

We serve as a vital bridge between hardworking farmers and active buyers, ensuring fair prices and completely honest dealings for all agricultural produce.

With years of trusted service to the farming community in Jalna district, our reputation is built on transparency, reliability, and our commitment to the prosperity of our farmers.

Licensed APMC Commission Merchant

Our Services आमच्या सेवा

Crop Receiving & Weighing

Accurate electronic weighment and safe storage of farmers' produce upon arrival at the Mondha.

Auction Facilitation
(Haroli / Lilav)

Conducting transparent open auctions to get the highest possible market price for your crops.

Fair Price Discovery

Leveraging market knowledge and a large buyer network to ensure you never undersell your yield.

Timely Payments

Prompt settlement of accounts and instant payment to farmers immediately after the sale is complete.
